The english word delivery has many meanings.
It can mean the action of delivering letters, packages, or ordered goods.
example: "allow up to 28 days for delivery"

It can mean an item or items delivered on a particular occasion.
example: "they are getting smaller deliveries"

In law, it can mean the formal or symbolic handing over of property, especially a sealed deed, to a grantee or third party.

It can mean the process of giving birth.
example: "injuries sustained during delivery"

It can mean an act of throwing or bowling a ball or striking a blow.
example: "a quick, compact delivery that sent the ball zinging"

It can mean the manner or style of giving a speech.
example: "her delivery was stilted"

Finally, It can mean the supply or provision of something.
example: "delivery of electricity at a specified price"
